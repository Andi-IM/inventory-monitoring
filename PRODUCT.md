# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Primary users are admins, technicians, and operational staff in labs, schools, or campuses that manage shared equipment.
They track inventory, approve borrowing requests, handle returns, and review item or transaction history.

## Product Purpose

HERTS exists to centralize equipment rental and tracking in one system.
It helps teams manage products, categories, containers, individual items, borrow/return workflows, approvals, exports, and audit trails with less manual coordination.

## Brand Personality

Practical, calm, precise.

The interface should feel reliable, organized, and efficient. It should reduce friction and make operational work feel controlled, not noisy.

## Anti-references

Avoid playful consumer app styling, generic SaaS dashboards, and visually loud admin themes.
Avoid cluttered tables, decorative noise, and layouts that make the borrowing workflow feel heavier than it is.

## Design Principles

- Make core inventory and borrowing actions immediately legible.
- Prioritize status, history, and approval states over decorative hierarchy.
- Keep dense operational data readable at a glance.
- Support fast scanning, verification, and decision-making.
- Make auditability and trust visible in the interface.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA as the baseline.
Support keyboard navigation, clear focus states, readable contrast, and forms that work well for frequent operational use.
Account for reduced motion, color vision differences, and multilingual content where needed.
